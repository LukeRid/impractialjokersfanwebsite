As the show kept on picking up steam and getting more popular they decided to try and get a movie deal which they got. Instead of the film being set in & around New York City they go road trip to Miami Florida to see a Paula Abdul show with 3 VIP tickets. To decide which three jokers got the three ticket on the road trip they participated in challenges on the way there and who ever lost didn’t get to go.

Whilst they are backstage at the show they got to meet Paula Abdul again and she offered them three tickets on a private jet that’ll take them back to New York from Miami. They go through the same process to decided witch three of them will go on the jet. The loser of challenges has to do a punishment before going back home.
